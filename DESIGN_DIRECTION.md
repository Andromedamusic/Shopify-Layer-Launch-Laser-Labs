## Core Design Principles

### 1. Tesla-Inspired Minimalism

**Key Takeaways from Tesla:**
- Large-scale hero images with product as the dominant visual
- Short, catchy slogans instead of walls of text
- High-quality images and videos showing products from multiple angles
- White/light text over dark imagery for contrast
- Auto-play video loops that add life without being distracting
- "Living minimalism" â€” clean themes with just enough motion to feel human

**Apply to LayerLaunch:**
- Hero section: Full-bleed slate coaster image with minimal text overlay
- Product pages: Large imagery, minimal copy, key features as icons
- Homepage: Let the product texture and engraving detail speak for itself
- Use subtle hover animations on product cards

### 2. Rivian-Inspired Premium Feel

**Key Takeaways from Rivian:**
- Design that "doesn't feel stale or dated" in its industry
- Connect users with relevant content through intentional information architecture
- Aspirational brand feeling translated to digital experience
- Material textures and craft quality visible in imagery
- Shopify integration for streamlined e-commerce

**Apply to LayerLaunch:**
- Showcase slate texture prominently (macro photography)
- Use imagery that shows the craft process (laser in action, hands holding product)
- Create an "adventure" narrative â€” these coasters go on journeys (weddings, homes, gifts)
- Navigation that guides without overwhelming

### 3. DTC Brand Excellence

**Key Takeaways from Premium DTC Brands:**

## Visual Language

### Color Palette

```
Primary:     #1A1A1A (Rich Black) â€” Headers, CTAs, text
Secondary:   #F8F8F8 (Soft White) â€” Backgrounds, cards
Accent:      #C9A227 (Warm Gold) â€” Highlights, badges, premium touches
Text:        #333333 (Dark Gray) â€” Body copy
Subtle:      #666666 (Medium Gray) â€” Secondary text, borders
```

**Rationale:**
- Black/white creates contrast that mirrors slate engraving (light cuts into dark)
- Gold accent signals premium/craft quality
- Warm tones prevent sterile feel

### Typography

```
Headings:    Montserrat (or similar geometric sans-serif)
             - Bold weight for impact
             - Generous letter-spacing on all-caps subtitles

Body:        Open Sans (or similar humanist sans-serif)
             - Regular weight for readability
             - 1.6-1.8 line height for comfort
```

### Photography Style

**Product Photography:**
- Clean, neutral backgrounds (white or light gray)
- Macro shots showing slate texture and engraving detail
- 45Â° angle shots showing depth and thickness
- Lifestyle shots in context (on coffee table, by drinks, in use)
- Process shots (laser engraving in action)

**Lighting:**
- Soft, directional light to emphasize engraving depth
- Slight shadows to ground products
- Avoid harsh flash or flat lighting

5. **Urgency Without Pressure**
   - "Made to order â€” please allow 5-7 days"
   - Low stock indicators (authentic, not fake)

## Micro-Interactions & Motion

**Tesla-inspired subtle animations:**

| Element | Animation |
|---------|-----------|
| Product cards | Scale 1.02x on hover, subtle shadow increase |
| CTAs | Background color transition (0.2s ease) |
| Images | Fade in on scroll (intersection observer) |
| Accordions | Smooth height transition |
| Cart icon | Subtle bounce when item added |
| Hero | Slow Ken Burns effect on background image |

**Rules:**
- Keep all animations under 0.3s
- Use ease-out timing for natural feel
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` setting
- No animations that block user interaction
